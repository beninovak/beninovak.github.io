The beginning

Our pizzeria was opened in 1921 by my great grandfather. He had been saving up for 6 years before gathering enough money to finally pursue his passion and open a pizzeria. My grandfather took over the business in 1959, my father in 1996, and since 2014 I have been keeping my grandfather's original vision going.

Where it all started

Our vision for the future

Every Benny's pizza employee is commited to contributing to the vision my great grandfather had all those years ago. To bring authentic, sustainable, and organic Italian food to everyone.
